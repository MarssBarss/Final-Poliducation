What is Poliducation?

Politics and democracy have garnered a vital position within modern society, with it being a source of peace and trouble at the same time. Nevertheless, society would be quite disfunctional without it. Because of this, as soon as young people - within the UK - reach age 18, they are given the option to vote. Being given the option to vote is definitely an empowering choice to have, knowing that you will ultimately affect the outcome of a referendum for once! However, it may seem daunting and frankly, quite difficult, to have to pick your political standing, free from any bias that may be imposed by family and friends. We at Poliducation realise that, so we formed a website meant to educate you on the political parties, completely free from bias.

It is purely informational, so that you may go off and make your own decision, or you can come here just to read up on the history and views of different parties!
